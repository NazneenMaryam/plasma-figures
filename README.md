# Broadband Laser–Plasma Interaction Figures

This repository contains the Python scripts used to generate the figures for the review paper:

**Controlling Nonlinear Parametric Instabilities in Laser–Plasma Interaction Using Broadband and Low-Coherence Lasers**

## Contents

## Figures

**Figure 1. Intermittent SRS bursts**
Conceptual illustration of intermittent stimulated Raman scattering bursts produced by temporal intensity fluctuations in a broadband laser. The figure was redrawn by the author based on the nonlinear burst mechanism discussed by Liu et al. [1].

**Figure 2. SBS reflectivity comparison**
Overall stimulated Brillouin scattering reflectivity for monochromatic and low-coherence laser conditions. The data were replotted by the author based on the SBS mitigation results reported by Zhao et al. [2].

**Figure 3. Coherence time versus laser bandwidth**
Coherence time calculated as a function of relative laser bandwidth using the relation τc = 2π/Δω. The figure was calculated and plotted by the author.

**Figure 4. Broadband-laser temporal intensity modulation**
Temporal intensity modulation of a broadband laser calculated using a polychromatic beamlet model. The figure was generated by the author based on the broadband-laser model and intensity-fluctuation mechanism discussed by Liu et al. [1].

**Figure 5. Density-dependent broadband BSRS behavior**
Qualitative description of the density-dependent behavior of broadband-driven back-stimulated Raman scattering. The figure was redrawn by the author based on the fluid and kinetic nonlinearities discussed by Liu et al. [3].

**Figure 6. Suppression of SRS by angular incoherence**
Effect of angular incoherence on stimulated Raman scattering suppression. The figure was replotted by the author based on the angular-incoherence results reported by Guo et al. [4].
